## Design principles
- Terminal / DOS aesthetic — keep it minimal and monochromatic
- No bracket `[LABEL]` wrapping on buttons
- Hero: name, title ("Software Engineer"), one dim subtitle line for specialization
- Project tags: languages and engines only (Rust, C++, C, C#, Java, GLSL, Unity, etc.) — no descriptive tags (PBR, Android, Multiplayer, etc.)
- Company logos: right-aligned in experience rows, grayscale, correct aspect ratio

## Experience / Education conventions
- Date format: `2023 - Present`, `2018 - 2023`, `2022` (single year)
- Spatial has two separate internship entries: 2019 and 2020
